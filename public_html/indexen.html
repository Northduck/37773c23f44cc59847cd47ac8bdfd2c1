Monument to Border Guards of the Northwest

Coming to the final stage creation of a monument to the "Border Guards of the North-West", which will be located in the Primorsky district of the city, on Savushkina street, opposite entrance to the Central Park of Culture and Rest named after S.M. Kirov.


The history of St. Petersburg is inextricably linked with the formation and development of domestic border authorities. Since ancient times, the lands on which our city stands, were the border of the Russian state. In St. Petersburg, the capital of the Russian Empire, was founded in 1893 A separate corps of border guards, here, on Vasilievsky Island, his headquarters was located. The 1st Petersburg border district OKPS.


Border guards have written many heroic pages in the defense Leningrad during the harsh years of the Great Patriotic War. In 1941 the approaches to the city were defended by about 20 border units, based on which formed the formations of the regular Red Army.

Today the structural divisions of the Border Directorate of the FSB Russia in the city of St. Petersburg and the Leningrad region are uncompromising fight against cross-border crime, smuggling and illegal migration, vehicles and goods, which make a significant contribution to the economy of the region and Russia as a whole.
